A Legacy of Impact and Leadership

Since its founding, ES International has been committed to raising leaders, empowering young people, and transforming communities through education, volunteerism, and social impact projects. What started as a small student initiative has grown into a global movement with chapters across schools and communities, nurturing the next generation of changemakers.

Who We Are?

ES International is a dynamic, youth-centered non-profit organization committed to raising a new generation of ethical, entrepreneurial, and impact-driven leaders across Africa. We believe that young people are not just the leaders of tomorrow they are the solution architects of today. Since 2005, we have been shaping minds, building capacity, and nurturing purpose in teenagers and young adults through structured creative engagement, leadership development, and entrepreneurial training. Our unique model blends literary expression, values-based mentorship, and enterprise education, reaching young people between the ages of 13 and 35 through school-based and community-centered clubs.

Our Vision

To build a continental legacy of thousands of morally grounded, globally relevant entrepreneurs and leaders by 2040, and to become a globally respected brand in youth empowerment and social development.
