Driven by Purpose.
Built for Impact.

  • To help young people explore, discover, and enhance their potential through literary activities such as debates, drama, and public speaking.
  • To instill a creative and entrepreneurial spirit in teenagers and youth.
  • To nurture the next generation of transformational leaders across Africa.
  • To develop sustainable platforms that promote creativity, innovation, leadership, and entrepreneurship among young people.
  • To extend humanitarian support and advocacy to promote equity, inclusion, and human dignity.

ES International was born from a desire to bridge the leadership and innovation gap among Africa’s youth.
We recognize that the future of our continent lies in equipping its youngest citizens with the tools, mindset, and opportunities to thrive and lead effectively in all spheres of life; education, business, politics, arts, and civil society.

  • Integrity – We walk in truth, even when it’s inconvenient.
  • Excellence – We pursue high standards in character and competence.
  • Morality – We build our work on ethical, spiritual, and cultural foundations.
  • Love for Humanity – We serve with compassion and empathy.
  • Inclusion – We create spaces where every voice and dream matters.
