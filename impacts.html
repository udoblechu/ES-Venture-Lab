TESTIMONIAL

It started as early as when I was in secondary school whilst serving in the teens arm of ES International known as "SLADDS". I had the opportunity to grow in public speaking, debating, and proper leadership, especially while serving as the Zonal President in charge of several secondary schools. That experience helped shape my confidence, communication skills, and sense of responsibility.

Beyond leadership, ES International also introduced me to the world of tech. As a beginner UI/UX designer, I received exposure, encouragement, and a strong foundation that sparked my passion for design. The support and opportunities I found here have set me on a clear path, and I’m confident this is only the beginning of how far I can go.

I’m deeply grateful to ES International for believing in me and giving me the platform to grow.

Oguh Evan C.
