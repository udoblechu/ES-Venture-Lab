TESTIMONIALS

Oguh Evan

My journey with ES International began in secondary school through its teens arm, SLADDS, where I developed strong public speaking, debating, and leadership skills, serving as Zonal President over several schools an experience that shaped my confidence, communication, and sense of responsibility. Beyond leadership, ES International also introduced me to tech, giving me my first exposure and solid foundation in UI/UX design, which sparked my passion for design. I’m deeply grateful for the support, belief, and opportunities ES International provided, as they have set me on a clear path and marked the beginning of how far I can go.
